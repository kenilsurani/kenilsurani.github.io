Immersion Heater – Laboratory Equipment Design

Freelance laboratory project (July 2020) — SolidWorks CAD modeling and KeyShot photorealistic rendering.

Project Overview

Designed a custom immersion heater assembly for a laboratory client. The focus was on creating a robust, manufacturing-ready 3D CAD model with clear representation of functional components, suitable for prototype development and technical communication.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Developed the complete 3D CAD model and assembly in SolidWorks.
  • Ensured accurate geometry and clean component integration for manufacturability.
  • Produced photorealistic renders in KeyShot for client review and presentation.

Tools Used

  • SolidWorks — 3D CAD modeling & assembly
  • KeyShot — photorealistic rendering

Key Outcomes

  • Delivered detailed CAD model and renders on schedule.
  • Supported clear communication of product intent for prototype and demonstration.
