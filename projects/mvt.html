Manual Variable Transmission (MVT) for Electric Vehicle

Academic Project | 2021–2022

Project Overview

Designed and developed a multi-ratio transmission system for electric two-wheelers to improve efficiency, climb performance, and acceleration compared to single-speed EV systems.

My Responsibilities

  • Complete 3D CAD modeling in SolidWorks
  • Created manufacturing drawings and BOM
  • Designed test platform for validation
  • Integrated transmission into EV prototype

Technical Highlights

  • Gear ratio range: 3.1 to 1.4
  • Improved climb performance by up to 40%
  • Extended vehicle range by approx. 10%
