12RU Data Rack Electrical Enclosure

Client: IP Enclosures Pty Ltd (Australia)
Date: November 2021
Standards: IP66, IK10, EIA-310-D, IEC/ENAS60529

Description

Designed a 12RU, 600mm deep industrial data rack enclosure for commercial and industrial environments. The enclosure was engineered to achieve IP66 ingress protection and IK10 impact resistance, meeting international rack and protection standards. The design focused heavily on durability, precision sheet-metal fabrication, and long-term reliability.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Complete 3D modeling of enclosure and all sheet metal components in SolidWorks.
  • Designed using 1.5mm galvanized steel for structural integrity.
  • Prepared full fabrication drawings with dimensioning and Bill of Materials (BOM).
  • Applied sealing strategy (polyurethane) and powder-coat finish for corrosion resistance.
  • Ensured compliance with EIA-310-D rack standards.

Software / Tools Used

  • SolidWorks – CAD modeling & Sheet Metal design
  • Manufacturing drawings and technical documentation

Key Outcomes

  • Delivered fabrication-ready documentation for high-volume industrial production.
  • Supported client’s product line with professional, standards-compliant technical drawings.
  • Maintained competitive industrial enclosure quality and reliability.
