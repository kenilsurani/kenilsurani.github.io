Spider Robot Ball for Fire Extinguisher Delivery

Client: Mr. Madhav Mansuriya
Duration: July 2020 – September 2020
Support: Startup India Innovation Fund

Description

Designed and prototyped a Spider Robot Ball capable of delivering a CO₂ fire extinguisher ball directly to the fire location. The robot uses a transformative eight-leg mechanism: it can walk with high flexibility like a spider and contract into a ball shape for rapid rolling—improving speed and accessibility in challenging environments.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Lead CAD designer: developed the full mechanical system from concept to finished prototype.
  • Engineered a spring-based launching system for targeted CO₂ ball deployment.
  • Integrated electronics: sensors, tracking camera, and Raspberry Pi controlled servo systems for navigation and actuation.
  • Coordinated prototype fabrication using advanced 3D printing for mechanical + electronics validation.

Key Technical Details

  • Custom mechanical assemblies designed (and checked) in SolidWorks.
  • Spring mechanism engineered for safe and reliable CO₂ ball launching.
  • Servo-driven multi-DOF leg joints for spider-like walking and fast ball transformation.
  • Full actuation + control logic implemented using Raspberry Pi microcontroller.

Key Outcomes

  • Built and tested a functional prototype with reliable movement and fire-extinguisher deployment in controlled conditions.
  • Demonstrated to innovation funding bodies; received positive technical validation and support.
