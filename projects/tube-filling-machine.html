Fully Automatic Plastic Tube Filling & Sealing Machine

Client: Punneli Technologies Limited (Dublin, Ireland)
Duration: Oct 2020 – Jan 2021
Company now operates in Australia as Punneli Pty Limited (Punneli).

Description

Designed a state-of-the-art automatic tube filling and sealing machine for the packaging industry. The machine efficiently fills, seals, and processes plastic tubes for high-volume production environments. Developed under the guidance of the company director, Mr. Nessen Paul. The project required designing custom mechanical components from the feed system to the sealing mechanism, ensuring manufacturability and robust, reliable operation.

Role & Responsibilities

  • CAD designer responsible for the end-to-end mechanical design process.
  • Modeled, assembled, and detailed every component in SolidWorks for full system integration.
  • Produced manufacturing drawings optimized for CNC, sheet metal, and machined fabrication.
  • Coordinated with the director and iterated designs based on technical feedback and production requirements.

Software / Tools Used

  • SolidWorks (machine design, assemblies, manufacturing drawings)
  • Animation preview + rendering tools (design validation and marketing)

Key Outcomes

  • Delivered a fully documented, production-ready design package for immediate fabrication.
  • Supported the client’s transition to automated packaging solutions (quality + efficiency improvements).
  • Fabricated components closely matched the CAD design (verified through photos/video).
