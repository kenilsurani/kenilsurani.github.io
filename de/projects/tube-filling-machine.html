Fully Automatic Plastic Tube Filling & Sealing Machine

Client: Punneli Technologies Limited (Dublin, Ireland)
Duration: October 2020 – January 2021
Company: Startup (now operates in Australia as Punneli Pty Limited)
Website: www.punneli.com
Guidance: Mr. Nessen Paul (Company Director)

Description

Designed a state-of-the-art automatic tube filling and sealing machine for the packaging industry. The machine efficiently fills, seals, and processes plastic tubes for high-volume production environments. Developed under expert guidance of the company’s director, Mr. Nessen Paul. This project required designing every custom mechanical component—from the feed system to the sealing mechanism— ensuring manufacturability and robust, reliable operation.

Role & Responsibilities

  • CAD designer responsible for the end-to-end mechanical design process.
  • Modeled, assembled, and detailed every component in SolidWorks for complete system integration.
  • Produced manufacturing drawings for each part, optimized for CNC, sheet metal, and machined fabrication processes.
  • Coordinated closely with the director, iterating designs based on technical feedback and production requirements.

Software / Tools Used

  • SolidWorks (machine design, assemblies, manufacturing drawings)
  • Animation preview and rendering tools (design validation and marketing)

Key Outcomes

  • Delivered a fully documented, production-ready design package for immediate fabrication.
  • Supported the client’s transition into automated packaging solutions, contributing to quality and efficiency advances.
  • Fabricated components closely matched the CAD design, verified through video and photographic records.
