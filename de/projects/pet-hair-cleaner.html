Pet Hair Cleaner – Product Design & Visualization

Client: Dino Lauando (USA)
Date: April 2021

Description

Designed two distinct variants of a pet hair cleaner intended for manufacturing and retail distribution. The product features an integrated roller, two roller blades, and two rubber blades. Roller blades keep the roller clean by sweeping collected debris into a waste tank, while rubber blades gather pet hair from fabric surfaces.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Developed product concepts and complete 3D CAD models for both variants.
  • Engineered internal blade configuration for cleaning performance and ease of maintenance.
  • Created photorealistic renderings for marketing and promotional use.

Software / Tools Used

  • SolidWorks – 3D CAD design and assemblies
  • KeyShot – rendering and visualization

Key Outcomes

  • Delivered high-quality renders showcasing aesthetics and functionality for product launch.
  • Supported client marketing and prototype development for mass manufacturing.
