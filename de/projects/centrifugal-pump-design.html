CENTRIFUGAL PUMP DESIGN & TECHNICAL ILLUSTRATIONS FOR PUBLICATION

Client: Kirit Domadiya – Service Solution Manager, Sundyne Middle East (June 2021)

Project Description

Provided comprehensive 3D CAD modeling and high-fidelity technical renderings of centrifugal pump systems to support professional technical publication. The work included detailed modeling of impeller geometry, volute casing, shaft arrangement, bearing support, and mechanical seal assemblies. Sectional cutaways and exploded configurations were developed to clearly illustrate internal flow paths and component interaction for educational and reference use.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Modeled complete pump assemblies in SolidWorks.
  • Developed precise cross-sectional and cutaway views.
  • Created publication-ready photorealistic renders.
  • Collaborated directly with industry author to align visuals with technical explanations.

Tools Used

  • SolidWorks – 3D CAD modeling & assemblies
  • KeyShot – High-quality technical rendering

Key Outcomes

  • Delivered accurate rotating equipment visualizations for publication.
  • Improved clarity of pump operation and mechanical seal explanation.
  • Strengthened practical understanding of industrial centrifugal pump systems.
